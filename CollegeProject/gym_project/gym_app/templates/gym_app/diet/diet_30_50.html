7-Day Diet Plan (Age 30–50) 🍇

Focus: Lean Muscle Maintenance, Heart Health, Energy & Recovery

🏋️‍♂️ Morning (Pre-workout - 6:00–7:00 AM)

5 soaked almonds & 2 walnuts
1 banana
Green tea or warm lemon water
Optional: Black coffee

💪 Post-Workout (8:00–8:30 AM)

1 scoop whey protein (if digestive system allows)
2 boiled eggs or sprouts bowl
Coconut water

🍽️ Breakfast (9:00–10:00 AM)

Vegetable oats or multigrain toast
1 boiled egg or paneer/tofu
1 fruit (apple/orange)

🍛 Lunch (1:00–2:00 PM)

1-2 multigrain roti or brown rice
Dal or grilled chicken
Mix salad (beetroot, carrot, cucumber)
Buttermilk

☕ Evening Snack (5:00–6:00 PM)

Roasted makhana or sprouts
Herbal tea

🍲 Dinner (8:00 PM)

Light meal: grilled paneer/tofu/fish
Steamed or sautéed vegetables
1 phulka or small portion of quinoa

🌙 Before Bed (9:30–10:00 PM)

Chamomile tea or warm turmeric milk
Optional: 1 tsp flaxseeds or chia seeds for digestion & heart health

📝 Notes

If you're aiming for weight loss, reduce carbs at dinner. If aiming for muscle gain, increase protein and healthy fats.
