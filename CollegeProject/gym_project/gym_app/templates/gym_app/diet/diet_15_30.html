7-Day Diet Plan (Age 15–30) 🌿

Focus: Muscle Gain, Endurance & Quick Recovery

🏋️‍♂️ Morning (Pre-workout - 6:00–7:30 AM)

1 banana or 1 apple
5–7 soaked almonds
Black coffee or green tea (optional)
1 scoop whey protein (if available)
1 boiled egg or brown bread with peanut butter

💪 Post-Workout (8:30–9:00 AM)

1 scoop whey protein in water or milk
2 boiled eggs or paneer/tofu
1 glass of lemon water with pink salt

🍽️ Breakfast (9:00–10:00 AM)

Oats or poha with vegetables
2 eggs or paneer bhurji
1 glass milk or smoothie (banana or mango)

🍛 Lunch (1:00–2:00 PM)

Brown rice/roti
Grilled chicken or lentils (dal)
Salad (cucumber, carrot, beetroot)
Curd/yogurt

🥜 Evening Snack (5:00–6:00 PM)

Handful of mixed nuts or roasted chickpeas
Fruit (apple/orange/berries)
Green tea or black coffee

🍲 Dinner (8:00–9:00 PM)

Roti or sweet potato
Grilled fish/chicken or tofu/paneer
Steamed vegetables or soup

🌙 Before Bed (10:00 PM)

1 glass warm milk with turmeric
Optional: 1 tbsp peanut butter or casein protein
