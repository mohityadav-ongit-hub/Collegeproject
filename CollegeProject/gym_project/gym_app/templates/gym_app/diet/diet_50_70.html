7-Day Diet Plan (Age 50–70) 🧓

Focus: Strength, Immunity & Digestion

🌅 Morning (6:30–7:30 AM)

1 glass warm water with lemon or 1 tsp soaked methi (fenugreek) seeds
5 soaked almonds + 2 walnuts
Light walk or yoga session (optional)

🍽️ Breakfast (8:00–9:00 AM)

Oats/porridge with seeds (chia/flax) and 1 banana
OR 2 multigrain toast with paneer/tofu or boiled egg
1 cup herbal tea or milk (low fat, if needed)

🥗 Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

Seasonal fruit (apple, papaya, orange, or guava)
OR coconut water

🍛 Lunch (1:00–2:00 PM)

1–2 multigrain rotis or 1 bowl brown rice
1 bowl dal or lightly spiced sabzi
Curd or buttermilk
Salad (cucumber, beetroot, carrot)

☕ Evening Snack (4:30–5:30 PM)

Roasted makhana or sprouts chaat
Green tea or herbal infusion

🍲 Dinner (7:30–8:30 PM)

Light sabzi (lauki, tinda, spinach, etc.)
1 phulka or small bowl of khichdi/dalia
Soup (tomato, moong, or mixed veg)

🌙 Before Bed (9:30 PM)

1 cup warm turmeric milk
Optional: 1 date or 1 tsp ghee if digestion is weak
